User Privacy

Eighth Period Signup Information

The eighth period signup information for students is by default only visible to TJ staff members and Intranet administrators. Without action by a student, this means that the activities they sign up for are not visible to other student users of Ion.


At all times, students retain the ability to see their name on their activity's roster.


Due to changes in FCPS policy, we do not offer a way for new students to grant permission for their eighth period information to become available to other students, and this text is only maintained for compatibility purposes.
